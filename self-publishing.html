BOOK PUBLISHING


WHD Publishing House offers services to the authors in assisting them to achieve their dreams of converting their manuscript into a Book.
We make it easy for any common man to publish their own book with our custom made Author Kits.

Formatting

The manuscript shall be paginated and made legible into a format of the book and the design the cover page that will be apt for the title and theme of the book

Proofing

The manuscript shall be ensured that it is proof read throughout the editing procedure and all the errors are nullified

E-Book

The final draft of the book shall be made into an exclusively E-Publishing Format so that it can be publishing in any E-Reading platform

Printing

The final book shall be printed into a paperback or hardback in the ideal dimensions that will aid the book

Online Launching

The book shall be launched online as in accordance how the client prefers to give a global face-off and international outreach

PR & Reviewing

After the launching, according to the client’s choice the reviewing assistance and press release process shall be catalysed
